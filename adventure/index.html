August 20, 2024

We have reached the sun. The goal is to slingshot around it and launch ourselves far into the solar system. The faculae sure are blinding being this close to the sun. I'm excited to carry on past it though. I've ranked the various celestial bodies we will visit in order from most excited to least excited. Saturn Jupiter Mars Uranus Venus Earth Mercury Moon Neptune Sun I can't wait to view the Kirkwood Gaps and see first hand if there really is an Oort Cloud. Faculae-Bright patches that are visible on the Sun's surface, or photosphere Kirkwood Gaps-Regions in the main belt of asteroids where few or no asteroids are found Oort Cloud-A theoretical shell of comets that is believed to exist at the outermost regions of our solar system IMAGE
